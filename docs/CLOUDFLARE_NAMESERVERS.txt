CLOUDFLARE NAMESERVERS FOR READYHOSTS.CO
=========================================

When you add readyhosts.co to Cloudflare, Cloudflare assigns TWO specific nameservers
to your domain. They are unique to your account — NOT the generic ns1.cloudflare.com / ns2.cloudflare.com
shown in old documentation.

After completing the "Add a Site" flow in Cloudflare, paste the assigned nameservers here:

  Primary:   __________________________.ns.cloudflare.com
  Secondary: __________________________.ns.cloudflare.com

Then enter both into GoDaddy:
  GoDaddy → Domain Portfolio → readyhosts.co → DNS → Nameservers → "I'll use my own nameservers"

IMPORTANT: This setup uses Cloudflare for DNS, NOT Vercel.
Cloudflare proxies traffic to Vercel via a CNAME or A record (configured inside Cloudflare).
Do NOT enter Vercel's nameservers (ns1.vercel-dns.com, etc.) into GoDaddy if using this setup.
